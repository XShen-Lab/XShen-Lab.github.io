Xiaohua Shen is a tenured professor at the School of Basic Medicine, Tsinghua University, and a senior investigator of the Tsinghua-Peking Center for Life Sciences.

Her research focuses on the noncoding genome, nuclear RNA, transcription, chromatin organization, nuclear architecture, and cell fate determination. The lab aims to understand how genetic information is interpreted and regulated in the three-dimensional nuclear environment.

## Appointment

- Tenured Professor, School of Basic Medicine, Tsinghua University
- Senior Investigator, Tsinghua-Peking Center for Life Sciences

## Education

- Ph.D., Biochemistry, University of Michigan Medical School, 2003
- B.S., School of Life Sciences, Nankai University, 1996

## Research Interests

- Noncoding genome and repetitive elements
- Nuclear RNA homeostasis
- Transcriptional regulation
- Chromatin folding and nuclear architecture
- Cell fate determination
