# Resources

The XShen Lab develops and maintains analysis pipelines, experimental protocols, and documentation for studying nuclear RNA, genome organization, transcriptional regulation, and cell fate.

Our resources focus on reproducible workflows rather than standalone software packages. Publicly available pipelines and protocols will be organized here as the lab website is updated.

{% include section.html %}

## Analysis Pipelines

### scFLUENT-seq analysis

Pipelines for analyzing single-cell nascent RNA sequencing data, including scFLUENT-seq-related workflows for studying sparse and probabilistic genome usage in individual cells.

### Nuclear RNA and transcription analysis

Workflows for analyzing nuclear RNA homeostasis, nascent transcription, RNA localization, and transcriptional regulation.

### Genome organization and chromatin analysis

Pipelines for studying chromatin architecture, repetitive elements, genome compartmentalization, and three-dimensional nuclear organization.

### Multi-omics and integrative analysis

Workflows for integrating transcriptomic, genomic, imaging, and chromatin-associated datasets to study genome information flow and cell fate regulation.
